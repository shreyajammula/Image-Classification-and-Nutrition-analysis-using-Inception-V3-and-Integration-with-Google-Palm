Fruits and Vegetable Classification:


Fruits and vegetables are essential components of a healthy diet, offering a diverse array of vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber crucial for overall well-being. Fruits, characterized by their sweet or tangy flavors, often contain high levels of essential nutrients such as vitamin C, potassium, and antioxidants. They contribute to improved immunity, digestion, and skin health. On the other hand, vegetables, with their savory or bitter profiles, provide vital nutrients like vitamin A, vitamin K, and folate, aiding in maintaining healthy vision, bone health, and cell regeneration. Regular consumption of a variety of fruits and vegetables promotes a balanced diet and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
